Facebook Friends

Add your friends as "FriendPerts" on the Facebook Friends page.

My FriendPerts

Create "My Tags" which will be assigned to different friends based on their interest/expertise in that tag.

Associate different "Tags" with different FriendPerts. An example:
  • Mom--@family, @wedding, @cooking, @hometown
  • Dad--@family, @fishing, @fantasyfootball
  • Brother--@family, @college, @fishing, @skiing
  • Sister--@family, @highschool, @dance
  • BestFriend--@college, @fishing, @mlb, @skiing

My Questions

Ask questions targeted at specific groups by assigning tags to your questions. FriendPerts that have that tag associated with them will get a notification under their "My Answers" section indicating that you have requested that they answer one of your questions.

My Answers

When a FriendPert answers your question, you will see a notification under your "My Questions" link indicating a new answer has been submitted for one of your questions.
